"Chess Piece" // Engineering

For this project we were taskde with creating a themed set of chess pieces on onshape. I chose to make the set based of off Candy Land characters.

Text-Based RPG // Engineering

The task for this project was to create a text based game with a minimum of 10 choices. I wanted to make an RPG game similar to something like DND.

Chemically Powered Car // Engineering (Group Project)

For this project we were tasked with making a chemically powered car. During the testing of the car we are challenged to see if we can hit a distance bullseye for the car by adding modifications to get as close as possible.

Inquiry Research // Humanities

At the beginning of the year our assignment was to create a research paper that we would work on incrementally through out the year. For our topic we were allowed to choose what every interested us.

Perils of Indiffernce // Humanities

For this assignment we had to research any non-profit of our choice to combat the perils of indifference. For this assignment I researched DrawChange.
